# White Associations

![*Fig 1.* Composite portrait II](/projects/white-associations/images/white-associations-image-2.jpg) ![*Fig 2.* My parents and me.](/projects/white-associations/images/white-associations-image-3.jpg)

![*Fig 3.* Composite portrait HNR](/projects/white-associations/images/white-associations-image-4.jpg)

In 2015, I moved from Brazil to the Netherlands. For the first time in my life I was racialised. There was an incredibly strong need for people to define who and how I looked like â€“ as to make their interaction with me and my racial "ambiguity" more comfortable to them.

*White Associations*, inspired by visual artist Nancy Burson's morphing composites, investigates white people's understanding of race and miscegenation, through the six celebrities (and one racist character) I was compared to.
